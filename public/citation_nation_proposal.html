A plain-English overview of the product, business model, and partnership paths. Designed for CEOs, partners, and investors who need clarity fast.

Product in one sentence

We turn traffic tickets into a clean digital case file and route it to the right attorney in minutes.

Citation Nation combines AI ticket analysis, guided intake, and a lawyer dashboard so firms can take more cases with less staff time.

Who it serves

  • Drivers: upload a ticket and get fast, clear next steps.
  • Law firms: receive qualified, structured cases with supporting evidence.
  • Partners: white-label or revenue-share access to the platform.

The problem

Ticket cases are profitable but messy to intake.

  • Manual intake wastes time and leads to dropped cases.
  • Firms struggle to qualify tickets quickly.
  • Drivers are confused and delay action, lowering conversion.
The solution

Automated intake with a case-ready output.

  • OCR + AI extracts violations, court dates, and state rules.
  • Guided intake validates missing info in real time.
  • Attorney dashboard shows status, documents, and next steps.
What a buyer gets

You are buying a revenue engine, not just software.

  • Platform access: full ticket intake workflow, analytics, and status tracking.
  • Case-ready output: structured summaries that reduce paralegal work.
  • Lead flow: inbound drivers routed into your firm or territory.
  • Brand leverage: optional white-label to present as your own service.

If you prefer a different structure (equity, license, or revenue share), we can align the deal accordingly.
